Who We Are

Personalised service, on schedule and on budget.

SML Enterprises focuses its attention on new construction, with planning as its primary area of expertise. Over the years the firm has established a quality reputation across earth works, residential buildings, apartments, hostel buildings, industrial buildings, sheds, pharma projects and roads work.

We offer personalised service for any size of project and maintain an experienced work force. We strive to complete projects on schedule and budget, while maintaining a smooth construction process and a quality finished product.

What We Do

Our core fields of activity

Five disciplines, delivered by one accountable team and one owned fleet.

Earth Works

Site excavation, cutting & filling, grading, rock breaking and bulk earth moving for industrial, refinery and highway projects.

Civil Works

RCC foundations, industrial slabs, sheds, buildings, helipads and finishing — from reinforcement to concreting.

Pipeline Works

Underground pipeline laying, trenching and allied works for oil & gas and utility infrastructure.

Mining

Quarry & mine excavation, rock loading and haulage with heavy excavators and tippers for bulk material extraction.

Machinery on Hire

Excavators, cranes, rollers, dumpers, man-lifts and support equipment supplied on hire — operated and maintained.
